Did You Know? 🐢
🛍️

A plastic bag can take 500 years to decompose in the ocean, breaking into microplastics that never fully disappear.

🐢

Sea turtles often mistake plastic bags for jellyfish. Ingesting just a single piece of plastic has a 22% chance of killing a sea turtle.

🌀

The Great Pacific Garbage Patch is now twice the size of Texas — an accumulation of 1.8 trillion pieces of plastic.

🍽️

Humans may be ingesting up to 5 grams of microplastic per week — roughly the weight of a credit card.
